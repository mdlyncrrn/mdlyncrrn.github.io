about me

I'm a Miami-based advertiser specializing in responsive and modern marketing through visuals. I've been studying at the Miami International University of art and design and I am 19 years old. I enjoy creating different styles and environments for many different people. Advertising is where I can work on a campaign that can be seen by the world and bring out various emotions. Graphic design is where I am able to express my mind through color schemes, forms, and texts to parade my concepts.
